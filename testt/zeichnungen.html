Drawing is my passion because...

I can dive in my own world and form it the way I want. There are no rules, no boundaries. I can just let my creativity play. Whatever I think of, I can draw. Also I am alone and it is so silent, I can just hear my pencil scratch over the thick paper. There is nothing holding me to the reality and I can just turn my mind off.
